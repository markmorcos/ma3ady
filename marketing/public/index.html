Common questions

Do my customers need an account?

No. They can book as a guest with just a name and email. They get a link to manage or cancel — no sign-up required.

Does it support Arabic out of the box?

Yes. The whole app — admin, booking, emails, reminders — is fluent in EN and AR with right-to-left layout.

How does pricing work?

ma3ady is free during the beta. When we introduce paid plans, we'll charge a flat monthly price — never per booking.

Can I take payments?

Not yet. v1 focuses on getting the booking flow right. Payments will arrive after we hear what tenants actually need.

What about my data?

Stored on Supabase (EU region by default), encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't sell anything to anyone. See the privacy policy for the details.
