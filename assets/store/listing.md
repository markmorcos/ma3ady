## 2. Full description — English (≤ 4000 chars)

```
ma3ady is the simplest way for small businesses — clinics, salons,
tutors, coaches, service pros — to take appointments without spending
half the day on the phone.

Send customers a link. They pick a service, see your real availability,
book in two taps. No app install required for them, no account creation,
no friction. You see every booking in the Today view, alongside what's
done, what's next, and what week you're having.

Built bilingual. The whole product — admin screens, public booking page,
emails, WhatsApp reminders — is fluent in English and Arabic with proper
right-to-left layout. Pick a language per business; customers see it in
their preferred locale.

WHAT YOU GET
• Today at a glance — your day on one screen with a "now" indicator
• Tap-to-edit availability — heatmap grid for your weekly hours, with
  per-day overrides for off-days, special hours, holidays
• Service catalog — duration, buffers, advance notice, daily caps
• Public booking link — yourname.ma3ady.com, share anywhere
• Email + WhatsApp reminders — automatic, on time, in the right language
• Manage-by-link — customers cancel or reschedule without an account
• Google sign-in for staff and owners — no passwords to forget
• Light / dark / system themes, dynamic type, screen-reader friendly

WHAT YOU DON'T GET (on purpose)
• No per-booking fees, ever
• No "premium reminders" upsell — reminders just work
• No bloat. We picked a small set of things to be excellent at, and we
  skipped the rest

PRICING
ma3ady is free during the beta. When we introduce paid plans, it'll be
a flat monthly price — never per-booking, never surprise charges.

PRIVACY
We collect the minimum: tenant name, customer name, email, optional
phone. No third-party trackers in the app. EU-region data hosting.
Right-to-be-forgotten on request. Full policy at
https://ma3ady.com/privacy.

WHO IT'S FOR
• Solo practitioners running their own schedule
• Small teams who book by the hour (salons, clinics, studios)
• Anyone tired of the back-and-forth of "what time works for you?"

WHO IT'S NOT FOR (yet)
• Businesses that need in-app payments
• Multi-location chains needing centralized reporting
• Staff-resource booking (one calendar per stylist / doctor)
We're working toward all of these. Today, the focus is on getting the
core booking flow right.

Try the demo: open the app and tap "demo" — no sign-up needed to see
the whole booking flow end-to-end.

Questions, requests, or bugs: <TBD: support@ma3ady.com>
```

Character count: ~2,150 / 4,000 — plenty of room to add quotes, more
features, or geo-specific copy.

## 4. App icon

`assets/store/icon-512.png` — 512×512 PNG, white clock mark on
`brand.500` teal (`#0F766E`). ✅ Play Store ready (Play wants 512×512
PNG, max 1 MB — current file is 28 KB).

**Gap for App Store:** Apple wants **1024×1024 PNG**, opaque, no
transparency, no rounded corners (Apple applies the mask). Upscale from
the existing SVG mark or re-export at 1024 from the source. Drop into
`assets/store/icon-1024.png`.

The source SVG marks are in `assets/branding/`:
- `mark.svg` — the icon-only clock-3 mark

| # | File                  | Screen                       | EN caption                       | AR caption                  |
| - | --------------------- | ---------------------------- | -------------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| 1 | `01-today.png`        | Today (admin)                | "Your day, one screen"           | "يومك في شاشة واحدة"        |
| 2 | `02-booking.png`      | Public booking — slot picker | "Customers book in two taps"     | "العملاء يحجزون بنقرتين"     |
| 3 | `03-reminders.png`    | Customer reminders feed      | "Reminders that arrive on time"  | "تذكيرات في وقتها"           |

**Why 1080×2160 and not 1080×2400 / 1080×2280:** Play Console rejects
screenshots where the longer side is more than 2× the shorter side.
1080×2160 sits exactly at the 1:2 limit and renders large enough on
modern phone-shaped previews in Play search results without losing
content to crop.

Three screenshots is the floor Play accepts (min 2, max 8) and it's
enough for a strong v1 listing. To expand the carousel later — a
heatmap availability shot, a manage-booking shot, a tablet split-view
— add a new HTML template under `scripts/store-assets/`, register it
in `capture.mjs`'s `SCREENS` array, and re-run.

## 10. What's left for you to do

1. Decide on publisher entity + support email — Play asks for both
   up-front and you can't submit without them. Fill the `<TBD>`s in §1
   and §8.
2. Confirm `https://ma3ady.com/privacy` is reachable in production (or
   tell me what URL to point Play at).
3. Submit through Play Console:
   - Upload the AAB (current production build flow already produces
     it via `build-android-local.yml` with `artifact-type: both`).
   - Paste app name / short / full description from §1–§3.
   - Upload `assets/store/icon-512.png` as the hi-res icon.
   - Upload `assets/store/play-feature-graphic.png` as the feature
     graphic.
   - Upload the three screenshots under
     `assets/store/screenshots/play/en/` in order.
   - In the localizations panel, add Arabic and upload the matching
     three from `assets/store/screenshots/play/ar/` plus the Arabic
     copy from §3.
4. After Play approves the upload key, append the upload-key SHA-256
   to `tenant-landing/src/app/.well-known/assetlinks.json` and
   redeploy tenant-landing so Android App Links keep verifying for
   Play-installed users.
5. Submit for review. First review usually takes 1–3 days.
